We help individual with health problem!

Yes we are not an average normal gyms, we are a complete gym with a team that looks to help and support anyone,

including the ones with physical problem or that need specific physioterapy exarcise to fast recover from any kind of injuries.

We supports individuals and groups with health problems and long term health conditions to lead an active, healthy life. Health Programmes delivers a range of activities to support people to self-manage their health conditions through physical activity and support those at risk of falling to improve their strength, balance and coordination. Each activity session is delivered by a qualified and experienced member of staff who have created a supportive and welcoming environment for all participants. If you have any queries about any of our Health Programmes or would like to know more about what support is available to enable you to be more physically active please don't hesitate to get in touch with us.
